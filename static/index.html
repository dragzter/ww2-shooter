You are the Lone Rifleman.

You are the last living member of your Rifle company defending a medical convoy. Your orders are to hold off the German attack at any cost. Do not let the enemy pass or you will be overrun and the men being evacuated will surely perish. The enemy is determined to fight to the bitter end. You must survive the onslaught in order for your brethren to survive.

Every time you repel a wave of infantry, they are sure to return with greater ferocity and numbers.

Much depends on your accuracy!

Select Difficulty and press "Start" to begin Round 1

The expected enemy strength will increase with every round.

You will have the opportunity to spend points on upgrades after round 1. Click the "SHOP" button in the round summary.
